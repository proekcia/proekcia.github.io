CUSTOMIZATION

GARMENT COLOR

Choose from 20 carefully selected colors, all garment-dyed after sewing for a rich color depth and soft feel. Additional wash treatments can create unique effects, including vintage fading, worn-in textures, and sun-bleached finishes.

Any garment from our collection can be dyed in any color from our palette — each shade has been carefully developed by our designers. Every color is truly one of a kind. For a custom color outside our palette, feel free to reach out to us.

ARTWORK TECHNIQUE

SILK SCREEN — A durable and vibrant printing method that applies ink directly to the fabric. Puff ink can also be used to create a raised 3D effect.

DTG (DIRECT-TO-GARMENT) — A digital printing method that produces highly detailed prints with smooth color transitions directly on the garment.

EMBROIDERY — A premium decoration method that uses thread to create a highly durable and professional-looking design.

WOVEN NECK LABEL

Complete your garments with custom woven labels featuring your brand logo. Labels can be produced in any size and color combination, creating a fully branded product down to the smallest detail.

HOW IT WORKS

1. GET IN TOUCH

Reach out through the website — send us a question or a full brief straight away. We respond the same day or the next morning, always.

2. BRIEF & APPROVAL

We go through every detail together — garment type, artwork, quantities, finish. If you already have sketches or a tech pack, even better. Once the final design and price are confirmed, we move forward.

3. PRODUCTION

Every piece is made to order — no excess, no waste. We work against fast fashion and take sustainability seriously. Each garment is cut, sewn and finished with the care that premium quality demands.

4. QUALITY CONTROL

Before anything leaves production, every unit is checked individually. Our quality standards are high — nothing ships until we are fully satisfied with the result.

5. PACKAGING & DELIVERY

Each garment is packed in branded zip bags designed for safe transit. We ship worldwide with trusted global carriers — fully tracked, every time.

FROM THE FOUNDER

For the past six years, I've dedicated myself to understanding every detail behind great garment creation — from the first sketch and fabric selection to the final stitch and finishing touch.

What began as a passion for design evolved into a deep commitment to craftsmanship, product development, and production excellence. Throughout this journey, I've learned that exceptional clothing is never the result of a single idea. Today, I work with brands, businesses, and creators to transform ideas into products that feel considered, purposeful, and built to last. My role is simple: to guide every stage of the process and ensure that every idea reaches its highest potential. Because the products I put my name behind should represent the very best of what they can be.

The minimum order quantity is 100 units per style. This allows us to maintain the production standards and garment-dye process that define the quality of our blanks.

From design approval to delivery, production takes approximately 21 days. We work with a focused range of styles and a reliable manufacturing partner in Portugal, which keeps timelines consistent.

You can order a blank sample in a standard base colour before committing to a full run. Samples are available without customisation — this lets you feel the fabric weight, assess the fit, and confirm the quality firsthand.

Every order is built around four parameters: colour (from our curated palette of 20 base shades), print method (screen printing, embroidery, or DTF), size run (XS–2XL), and branded label. All customisation happens on top of our fixed blank silhouettes — we don't alter the cut or construction.

We offer a palette of 20 carefully selected base colours. Each shade is applied via garment dyeing after the piece is sewn, which produces a naturally rich, premium finish. Custom colours outside the palette can be discussed individually.

Garment dyeing means the finished piece is dyed after it's been sewn — not cut from pre-dyed fabric. This results in a more even dye uptake, subtle tonal variation at the seams that adds depth and character, and a pre-washed feel that won't shrink or lose shape after washing. It's one of the key markers of premium production.
